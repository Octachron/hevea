\section{Examples of proof.sty}

\verb|\infer| draws beautiful proof figures easily:

\noindent(1)
$$
\infer={A}{
        \infer[\imp E]{B}{
                \frac{\Pi}{2}
                &
                \infer[\wedge E$_1$]{B21}{B21 \wedge B22} 
                & 
                D1 \wedge D2
        }
        &
        \infer{C}{C11 \wedge C12}
}
$$

\noindent(2)
$$
\infer{A}{
        \infer*[\imp E]{B}{
                \frac{\Pi}{2}
                &
                \infer*[\wedge E$_1$]{B21}{B21 \wedge B22} 
                & 
                D1 \wedge D2
        }
        &
        \deduce{C}{C11 \wedge C12}
}
$$

\noindent (3)
$$
\infer*[RI]{A \imp B}{
        \infer{B}{[A]}
}
$$

\noindent (4)
$$
\infer[(1)]{A}{
        \infer*{B}{
                B11\land B12\land B13
                &
                B21\land B22\land B23
        }
        &
        C
}
$$

\noindent (5)
$$
\infer[(\land I)]{A \land B}{A & B}
\qquad
\infer[(\land E$_l$)]{A}{A\land B}
\qquad
\infer[(\land E$_r$)]{B}{A\land B}
$$
